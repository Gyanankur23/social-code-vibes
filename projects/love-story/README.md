# 💕 Love Story

An interactive 3D love story experience with particle effects, smooth animations, and engaging interactions.

## ✨ Features

- **Smooth Card Transitions** - Instant, buttery smooth navigation between story sections
- **Particle Effects** - Floating hearts and particle burst animations
- **Present Opening** - Interactive gift boxes with surprise reveals
- **Fully Responsive** - Works perfectly on mobile, tablet, and desktop
- **Touch Optimized** - Designed for Android, iOS, and all touch devices
- **Production Ready** - Optimized performance, no dependencies

## 🎯 How It Works

1. Click "Begin Our Story" to start
2. Answer questions with Yes/No options
3. Open surprise presents to reveal gifts
4. Experience the final love message
5. Restart to experience again

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **HTML5** - Semantic markup
- **CSS3** - Modern animations, gradients, and effects
- **JavaScript** - Vanilla JS, no dependencies
- **Canvas API** - Particle system
- **CSS Animations** - Smooth transitions and effects
